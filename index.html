About

I was born in Quezon City and spent 5 short years in the Philippines. Entering Grade 1 my family moved to Papua New Guinea and, while the transition was a little rough, managed to assimilate ourselves well with our new environment and becoming a part of the small OFW Filipino community. During my stay in Papua New Guinea, i moved from various schools including St Josephs International, Ela Beach International, Murray International and finally Port Moresby International School completing grades 1-12 throughout these schools. It was during my time here that i learned the values and ideals i have today about racism and discrimination and becoming tolerant of other people in all walks of life. It was also during my time in PNG that i learned to appreciate IT, History and World Literature becoming the foundation of many of my hobbies and interests.



I have many interests and hobbies including reading novels such as the Harry Potter Series and classics such as the Lord of the Flies. I also read Manga such as One Piece, Naruto and Rurouni Kenshin. I also enjoy watching anime like Code Geass and Steins Gate and your typical tv series such as Game of Thrones, Daredevil and Vampire Diaries. I am also health-oriented and like to watch what i eat and try to at the very least exercise/job/gym regularly. I also consider myself a hardcore gamer with a focus on strategy and RPG games such as EUIV, CK2, The Total War series, The Witcher series, The Final Fantasy series and many more.

Currently i am a student in the University of the Cordilleras taking up my undergraduate course for Information Technology under the ERP track
